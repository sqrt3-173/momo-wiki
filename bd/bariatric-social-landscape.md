# Bariatric social-media landscape (BD research, started 2026-07-04)

Eli's task: find the top ~50 bariatric **dietitians / clinics / surgeons** by Instagram + TikTok
following + engagement, analyse their content (post types, engagement inflection points), organise

## KEY STRATEGIC FINDING (the valuable one)
**Bariatric social is a thin niche — and Australia is almost wide open.** Exactly ONE AU account has
real size (Jane Stoltze, dietitian, ~25K). Every other AU surgeon/clinic is <5K, most <1K; even
A/Prof Talbot (top-credentialed Sydney bariatric surgeon) has no real social presence. → **NV Health
could own AU bariatric social with little competition.** Likely worth more than the content teardown.

## DATA CONSTRAINT (confirmed, not theoretical)
Instagram, TikTok, AND Social Blade all block WebFetch (403 / JS-rendered). All follower numbers below
are ESTIMATES from search snippets / third-party influencer DBs, several likely stale. **Per-post
engagement-timeline analysis ("where did it skyrocket") genuinely needs a social-analytics tool**
(HypeAuditor / Apify / Social Blade paid). Asked Eli if he has one — that's the fork for analysis depth.
